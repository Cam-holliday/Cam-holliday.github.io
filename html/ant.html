A story about an ant

An ant’s life is one of rigor and pain. Dude, the ant’s name, is the hottest ant there is. He gets out of most work because he is also a stupendous persuader,which is a good trait to have. On this one occasion, however, Dude had to actually do something. He was asked to go out around the spider’s den and bring back a fly’s wing. Naturally, he was chosen because everybody hated him by this point except for the young ladies, who had no say in the food gathering schedule. As he exited the entrance to the ant cave he became immediately frightened by all the action around him. From the birds to the worms he was by far the smallest creature that crawled on the earth. His size, in this case, was his strength in this situation, since nothing saw him. He easily snuck past the lazy spiders and made his way to the fly's wing, which was his primary target. He grabbed the wing and made the slow journey back to his home, that he had no clue how the wing would fit into, but he kept going. Right as he was getting to the house he felt a tug on the wing, and the next thing you know he is gone with the wind.

A story about an ant Pt. 2

As the ant got carried away he remembered his training of How to Escape All Situations by father ant. First he let go of the fly’s wing and fell to the ground. Then he remembered that was the only thing that the book said to do. Now, however, he needed some food for his family of ants, but he had no clue where he was or how to get home, since he was so short. So he turned on his internal compass and followed it home. Now that he knew where home was he set off again to find food. Walking passed the spiders home, Dude found that one of the spiders had lost a leg. His new adventure began. As expected, he cautiously snuck into their home and grabbed the leg as stealthy as a ninja. Also in the process of his sneakiness he forgot to silence his phone, which sounded like an air horn in the quiet spiders home. All the spiders were up and Dude was on the run.

A story about an ant Pt. 3

Scared for his life, Dude ran as fast as his little legs could carry him, which wasn’t fast at all. The spiders spotted him and ran after Dude, but luckly dude found a little crack in the ground where the spiders couldn’t reach him. Now, however, he had to wait until the spiders got bored or until they found new food. Dude waited. TIme passed and it started to get dark, but the spiders stayed. A little while later they started to fall asleep right where they stood and blocked him in. Dude was left with no choice but to explore his crack in the ground. Walking slowly and cautiously, Dude found a tunnel that led to another tunnel, and soon enough he was lost, alone, and afraid. He kept walking, though, because he had no other choice, and after he had lost all hope he started to hear noises. He got closer and closer, and eventually he came across an opening in the ground. He looked into the hole of light and found that he was looking down at a funeral. Then he realized that it was his funeral, but then suddenly he slipped and fell right into his own coffin. There were mummers, screams, and laughs, but then everyone was happy and welcomed him back with glee.
